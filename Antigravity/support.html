Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

What documents do I need for purchasing a bike?

You need a valid ID proof (Aadhaar/PAN), address proof, and passport-size photographs. For financing, additional income proof may be required.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we offer doorstep delivery for all our bikes. Contact your nearest dealer for more details.

What is the warranty period?

All our bikes come with a standard 2-year warranty or 30,000 km (whichever comes first). Extended warranty options are also available.

How long does the test drive take?

A typical test drive lasts 30-45 minutes, giving you ample time to experience the bike.

Do you offer exchange programs?

Yes, we have attractive exchange offers. Bring your old bike to any dealership for a free evaluation.

What are the charging options for electric bikes?

Electric bikes can be charged at home using a standard 15A socket. We also provide information about public charging stations.
